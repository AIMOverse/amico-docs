## For Developers

If you're interested in contributing to Amico, we welcome pull requests and issues.

## Join Our Community 

We believe in building together and value input from developers, enthusiasts, and users. Join the conversation, share ideas, and collaborate with other contributors.

Telegram: Connect with our community on [Telegram](https://t.me/aimoverse) for discussions, updates, and support.
Twitter/X: Follow us on [Twitter/X](https://x.com/AIMOverse) for the latest news and announcements.
GitHub: Star our repository and track development on [GitHub](https://github.com/AIMOverse/amico).

Your contributions help shape the future of Amico! Whether you're coding, testing, or sharing feedback, every effort counts.
